Specialist Endodontist

Dr. Aline Saade

A specialist endodontist dedicated to one discipline and one mission: preserving natural teeth through precision, evidence, and compassion.

Dr. Aline founded Dental Experts Center in Abu Dhabi with the belief that every tooth deserves a chance. Today, DEC is a JCI-accredited, multi-specialty clinic with a 4.9-star Google rating. With more than two decades of specialist experience — including her role as Chair of the Endodontic Subcommittee at SEHA — she brings both clinical depth and leadership to every aspect of her practice.

The Journey

Building a Career in Endodontics

1992 – 2001
Saint Joseph University of Beirut

DCD – DESE Specialist in Endodontics/Endodontology. Nine years of rigorous training at one of Lebanon's most prestigious universities, culminating in a specialist degree and a master's thesis on apical third anatomy.

1997 – 2002
Private Practice — Lebanon

Established a successful dental practice recognised for excellence in general and specialised endodontic care. Developed a referral network with local dentists for complex cases, becoming a trusted specialist in the community.

2002 – 2010
Full-Time Endodontics Specialist — Dr. Munir Silwadi Dental Center

Specialised in micro-endodontics, significantly improving success rates of complex root canal treatments. Participated in community dental health programmes, providing endodontic care and education to underserved populations.

2010 – 2015
Specialist Endodontics & Chair of Endodontic Subcommittee — SEHA

At SEHA Ambulatory Healthcare Services, directed the development of endodontic protocols standardising treatment procedures across the organisation. Conducted training workshops for dental staff and led quality assurance initiatives monitoring treatment outcomes.

2015
Dental Experts Center Founded — Abu Dhabi

Founded Dental Experts Center in Abu Dhabi — a multi-specialty clinic built on the principle that patients deserve comprehensive, evidence-based dental care under one roof. Located on the 20th floor of Das Tower, Al Bateen Street.

Accreditation
JCI Accreditation Achieved

Dental Experts Center earned Joint Commission International accreditation — the gold standard in healthcare quality and patient safety. A milestone that validated the clinic's protocols and commitment to excellence.

Today
25+ Years · Owner & Clinic Manager · Pierre Fauchard Academy Fellow

More than two decades of specialist endodontic experience. Continuing to advance the field through clinical research, published work, and a commitment to delivering exceptional patient care while optimising clinic operations.
