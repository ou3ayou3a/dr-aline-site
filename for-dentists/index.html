Thank you for considering Dr. Aline Saade for your endodontic referrals. A well-coordinated referral protects your patient relationship while ensuring specialist-level outcomes for complex cases.

Why Refer

A Partner, Not a Replacement

Specialist Expertise

Microscopic endodontics, CBCT diagnostics, and bioceramic materials. Complex cases — calcified canals, retreatments, resorption, apicoectomy — handled with precision.

Clear Communication

Same-day updates after each appointment. Detailed treatment reports with microscopic images, radiographs, and prognosis assessment — sent directly to you.

Patient Returns to You

Once endodontic treatment is complete, your patient is returned to your care for restorative follow-up. Your relationship stays intact. Always.

How It Works

The Referral Process

1
Submit a Referral

Use the online referral form, call, or email. Include patient details, tooth number, clinical findings, and any radiographs.

2
Patient Contact Within 24 Hours

Your patient is contacted promptly to schedule an appointment. Urgent and emergency cases are accommodated on the same day.

3
Treatment & Documentation

Treatment is completed under the operating microscope with full documentation. You receive an update after each appointment.

4
Detailed Report to You

A comprehensive report including diagnosis, treatment rendered, materials used, microscopic images, and recommended restorative timeline.

5
Patient Returned for Restoration

Your patient returns to your practice for the permanent restoration. We include specific recommendations for the restorative approach.
