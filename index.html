About Dr. Aline

A Career Dedicated
to Saving Teeth

From early training in endodontics to founding one of Abu Dhabi's most reviewed dental clinics, Dr. Aline Saade has built her career on a single conviction: a natural tooth, properly treated, can last a lifetime.

As a specialist endodontist, Dr. Aline focuses exclusively on root canal treatment, retreatment, and endodontic surgery. She combines operating microscope magnification with advanced biomaterials to treat even the most complex cases — from calcified canals to resorption to trauma.
